Tracking deforestation rates is essential for understanding its impact on biodiversity. This table highlights the deforestation statistics across different regions.

About This Data

This table presents deforestation rates and the primary causes of forest loss across different global regions. It highlights the protection efforts being made and the overall status of deforestation in each region. Understanding these statistics helps in assessing global conservation strategies and their effectiveness in achieving the UN Sustainable Development Goal 15—protecting, restoring, and promoting sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems.

Table Legend

  • ✔ Available or Completed → Indicates stable or improving conditions.
  • ✖ Not Available or Incomplete → Indicates worsening conditions.
